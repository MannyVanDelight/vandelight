Visual Alchemist & Creative Director

Manny Van Delight

Did I do well mum?


A creative rascal. A world-builder. A storyteller who turns distant lands and bold ideas into engaging art. Guided by my inner child as my true north, I fuse photography, film, and visual effects magic into powerful, immersive experiences.


For decades, I crafted visuals for the world’s biggest brands - Audi, Porsche, Toyota -and contributed to films like Avatar, Tintin, Wonder Woman, Tomb Raider, and games like Horizon Zero Dawn. But beyond the industry, I found my true passion: creating my own art, where storytelling and technology collide.


Now, I’m pushing the boundaries of interactive filmmaking -blending visuals, narrative, and cutting-edge tech to create stories where the audience plays a role. My work isn’t just something to watch; it’s something to experience and shape. Through community-driven storytelling, I invite people to vote and influence the narrative, making them an integral part of the creative process.


And while I continue to carve my own path, I’m also working on one of the biggest movies out there - though I can’t reveal what it is just yet. Every share, every kind word, every purchase fuels this journey and for that, you have our deepest gratitude. - MvD
